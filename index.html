What is IEEE RAS?

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society is the leading global organization for advancing innovation in robotics, automation, and intelligent systems through research, education, and collaboration.

🤖

Robotics

Advancing robotic systems from industrial automation to humanoid robots, pushing the boundaries of mechanical intelligence and human-robot interaction.

⚡

Automation

Developing intelligent automation solutions that transform industries, from manufacturing and logistics to healthcare and smart infrastructure.

🧠

AI & Machine Learning

Integrating artificial intelligence and machine learning with robotics to create adaptive, learning systems that solve complex real-world challenges.

OUR CHAPTER

About IEEE RAS MITS

The IEEE RAS Student Chapter at Madhav Institute of Technology & Science (MITS), Gwalior was established to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical innovation in robotics and automation. As a Deemed University, MITS provides the perfect ecosystem for fostering technical excellence.

Our chapter brings together passionate students who are driven to explore cutting-edge technologies, participate in global competitions, and contribute to the advancement of robotics and AI.

  • Hands-on workshops and technical training
  • Global networking through IEEE's worldwide community
  • Competitive robotics and hackathon participation
  • Research mentorship and publication support
  • Industry collaboration and career development

Our Mission

To foster a community of innovators and technologists by providing hands-on experience in robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence, while connecting students with IEEE's global network of professionals, researchers, and industry leaders.

🔭

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized student chapter known for excellence in robotics education, impactful research contributions, and developing industry-ready talent that shapes the future of technology and automation.
