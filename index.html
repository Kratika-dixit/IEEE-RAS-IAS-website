Who We Are

We are a passionate community of students dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology. Our branch unites the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) and the Industry Applications Society (IAS) to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world industrial practice.

Our Mission

To empower students with technical knowledge, innovation opportunities and leadership experiences.

Our Vision

To create future-ready engineers who advance technology for humanity.
