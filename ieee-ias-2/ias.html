Who We Are

About IEEE IAS

The Industry Applications Society chapter at MITS Gwalior, driving real-world innovation in electrical, power and industrial engineering.

Our Mission

To promote the application of electrical and electronics engineering across industry through innovation, research and technical excellence. We provide hands-on learning, workshops and collaborative platforms that empower students to connect academia with real-world industrial challenges.

  • Power Systems
  • Industry 4.0
  • Electronics
  • Research
  • Workshops

Our Vision

To lead the way toward a sustainable, technology-driven industrial future. We aim to build industry-ready engineers with strong research culture, leadership and engineering excellence, prepared to solve real-world challenges at scale.

  • Leadership
  • Industry Ready
  • Excellence
